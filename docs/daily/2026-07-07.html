💡 Today's Take

The strongest signal today is that **the AI industry is transitioning from a "model arms race" to a "capability deployment race."** On one hand, products like Tencent Hy3 and Zhipu ZCode show rapid optimization of open-source model and AI coding tool price/performance, clearly benefiting developers. On the other hand, Anthropic's "global workspace" discovery and Nvidia's hardware delays remind us from both software and hardware sides that the industry's infrastructure and cognitive boundaries are far from stable. **Agent security threats moving from theory to reality** (JADEPUFFER ransomware) and **declining model tool call reliability** (Better Models, Worse Tools) are two signals all AI builders should watch closely—as models grow stronger, system vulnerabilities amplify in parallel.
